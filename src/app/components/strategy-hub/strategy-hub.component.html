TikTok Protocol

  • Post 15–30 second hook clips daily for 7 days
  • Use trending audio overlays on instrumental cuts
  • Drive saves—saves trigger the algorithm, not just views
  • Best posting times: 7–9 AM and 7–10 PM local

Instagram Protocol

  • Reels perform 3× better than static posts in reach
  • Use Stories for behind-the-scenes studio content
  • Tag collaborators and producers to multiply reach
  • Link Spotify pre-save in bio during release window

YouTube Protocol

  • Upload Shorts version of every release (60 sec max)
  • Full music video drives long-term search discovery
  • Optimize titles with genre keywords for SEO
  • Enable monetization to capture YouTube Music streams
